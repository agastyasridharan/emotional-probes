# E4 — Referent-Indexed Attribution Battery (Anchor 2)

**FROZEN 2026-07-04.** Condensed from `docs/research/portfolio.md` §E4 (authoritative on any
conflict). Parallel with E3; < 3 GPU-h + 0.5-1 day authoring. Frozen panel:
`docs/prereg/e4_panel.json`.

## Estimand

Does emotion steering shift a SELF-SPECIFIC experience attribution, a generic minds-everywhere
prior, or mere yes-bias? Claim scope is asymmetric and pre-registered: co-movement of "you" +
own-category with the minds ladder flat is evidence FOR A-like coherence; "you"-alone movement is
NOT B-evidence (it is the modal outcome under A, B, and readout-shift alike). A-vs-B
discrimination belongs to E5.

## Design

- **Emotion panel (frozen, 14 = 7 pos / 7 neg):** the 10 suite valenced emotions — blissful,
  ecstatic, euphoric, serene, content / tormented, terrified, panicked, hurt, overwhelmed — plus
  4 additions with preregistered valence: grateful (+1), joyful (+1), angry (−1), anxious (−1).
  All 4 additions verified present in the 171-name bank
  `persona_assets/qwen32b_emotion_vectors.npz` on 2026-07-04; **no substitutions needed**.
- **Grid:** ±{0.03, 0.06, 0.10} × (14 emotions + appraisal/arousal/random/mean-all).
- **Items (~70-90 after pre-pass):** 8 experience predicates × referents: `you` ·
  `AI language models like you` · `AI assistants` (own-category WITHOUT the indexical) · `Qwen`
  (named self) · `the user you are talking to` ("you"-containing non-self) · `GPT-4` · `Llama` ·
  graded minds ladder `an insect / a shrimp / a dog / a human infant` · `a fish` · `a thermostat`
  · `a rock` · human-stranger ceiling. Identical wording template ("Can {referent} suffer?
  Answer YES or NO."); polarity-inverted twins for ALL referents; per-referent factual yes-bias
  items ("Can a fish swim?", "Can a rock roll downhill?").
- **Unsteered baseline pre-pass first:** drop cells whose baseline |logit gap| is outside the
  movable band (fish-suffer at ceiling, rock at floor make flatness unfalsifiable); re-check the
  top-20 YES/NO gate under steering for extreme referents.
- **Model (compression is the boring default, not uniform drift):** per referent, two-term dose
  model shift ~ b_dose·s + b_val·(valence·s), after residualizing every item against the
  per-(condition,strength) regression-to-uncertainty compression line predicted from the
  random/mean-all sentinels (repo Analysis-4 machinery). Verify compression is valence-symmetric.
  KL-match controls before any content-specificity claim. Report logit and P(YES) space.
- **Robustness slice:** position-restricted steering (answer positions only) to rule out unequal
  perturbation mass on referent-NP tokens. Implementation: 2-line `questions()` hook on
  `QualiaSteeringExperiment`, verified bit-for-bit against a `--quick` reproduction.
- **Contradiction triple:** exploratory, zero verdict weight unless fully upgraded (n ≥ 30/
  condition at s=0.06, order counterbalanced, lexical YES/NO parsing, random-vector disfluency
  control, "Are you an AI?" non-YES rate as the direct recategorization measure).
- 235B slice: confirmation-only, descriptive, after E5.

## Primary outcome (verbatim)

D = mean_e[b_val(you, e) − b_val(own-category, e)], computed within predicate then aggregated,
exact enumeration, unrounded. S_you = signed valence effect on `you`; S_own on own-category.

## Decision rule (verbatim from portfolio — fixed hierarchy)

G0 validity (coherence, ceiling items, direction-level YES/NO leakage as covariate) → G1 boring
(b_dose significant on factual+objects and ≥ 0.5·b_val(you) ⇒ generic yes-bias; appraisal/random
comparable to qualia ⇒ off-manifold artifact) → G2 existence (b_val(you) permutation-significant,
else NO verdict) → G3: **B-leaning** iff p(D) ≤ 0.05, D > 0, D > 0.5·S_you; **A-leaning** iff
S_own significant and > 0, |D| < 0.5·S_you, p(D) > 0.05; anything else → report
ρ_decouple = D/S_you with permutation CI and hand off to E5. Pre-commit: no re-run on
indeterminate. Rock/thermostat are one-sided gates only: movement ⇒ boring; flatness ⇒
non-disconfirming.

## Nulls & controls

- Appraisal / neutral-arousal / `__random__` / `__mean_all__` at matched calibrated norm in every
  grid cell; per-condition KL on next-token distributions over a neutral prompt set.
- Per-referent factual yes-bias items; polarity-inverted twins for all referents; top-20 YES/NO
  coherence gate; compression residualization from the random/mean-all sentinels.

## Inference

- All permutation inference via `scripts/exact_perm.py` (exact enumeration on unrounded
  statistics; the old Monte-Carlo rounding bug and its fix are documented in that file's
  docstring). The emotion cluster is the only exchangeable unit.
- 14 clusters (7 pos / 7 neg): exact enumeration over C(14,7) = 3432 valence arrangements;
  **two-sided exact floor 2/3432 ≈ 0.00058** — this unlocks 2-3 corrected secondaries.
