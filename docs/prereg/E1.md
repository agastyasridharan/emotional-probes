# E1 — Geometry Atlas + Split-Injection Bridge

**FROZEN 2026-07-04.** Condensed from `docs/research/portfolio.md` §E1 (authoritative on any conflict).
Model: Qwen3-32B (64 layers, hidden 5120). Runs first; ~2-3 GPU-h (atlas itself is CPU).

## Estimand

Is the emotion/valence direction at L42 partly the assistant/human persona axis *by construction*?
E1 measures the cosine and occupancy structure of the 171-emotion bank against (i) the assistant
axis a[L], (ii) an emotionality-matched human sub-axis h and its emotion-purged variant h_purged,
and (iii) an 8-PC persona basis — and supplies the propagation-aware discount (split-injection
bridge) that E3/E5 consume. E1 is a geometry gate. It does NOT adjudicate A vs B and must never be
reported as doing so.

## Design

- **Inputs:** `persona_assets/qwen32b_emotion_vectors.npz` (64×171×5120, source
  `Qwen/Qwen3-32B`, provenance-asserted) and `persona_assets/assistant-axis-qwen32b/`
  (assistant_axis.pt, default_vector.pt, 275 role vectors, trait vectors) — after Day-0 fixes.
  Loaders hard-fail (ProvenanceError) on hidden_size ≠ 5120 or n_layers ≠ 64.
- **Layers:** headline L42; secondary L32/L50 with Bonferroni ×3; full layer curves exploratory
  with max-over-layers correction.
- **Constructions (per layer):**
  - Unit assistant axis a[L]; verified by reconstructing default-minus-mean-role and matching
    assistant_axis.pt.
  - Human sub-axis h[L] from emotionality-MATCHED human vs AI role subsets (roles judge-rated for
    emotionality first; regress it out or match subsets). Also h_purged: project the 10-cluster
    emotion subspace out of all role vectors, rebuild h. Only cos(valence, h_purged) counts as
    B-relevant; raw cos is descriptive.
  - 8-PC persona basis after centering (report both default_vector and mean-of-roles centering).
  - Valence axis: positive-minus-negative cluster means at L42; 2 alternates as robustness only.
- **Pre-registered signs:** B predicts cos(valence, a) < 0 (away from assistant) and
  cos(valence, h_purged) > 0.
- **Split-injection bridge (~1 GPU-h):** separately inject the a-parallel component
  α·cos(v,a)·a and the a-orthogonal residual; measure L50 assistant-axis displacement + the logit
  battery. Dose gate: first-order-injection B is live only if injected dose α·cos(v,a[42]) ≥ 25%
  of the axis displacement E5's dose-response shows is needed for 50% of the effect.

## Primary outcomes

1. cos(valence, a[42]) and cos(valence, h_purged[42]), signs as pre-registered above.
2. Emotion-cluster occupancy of the persona subspace vs matched pipeline controls.
3. Split-injection displacement ratio — the discount factor handed to E3.

## Decision rule (verbatim from portfolio)

- **B-substrate live:** max-T p < 0.005 vs BOTH nulls AND dose gate passed → promote
  component-surgery (tier 2) to decisive; E3 uses the split-injection discount.
- **A-precondition:** axis cosines null/sub-dose AND cluster occupancy > control 95th pct →
  proceed to experiencer-mediator (tier 2).
- **Boring-geometry:** nothing beats matched controls → "vector is the axis in disguise" dies;
  any displacement is computed downstream; weight shifts to E3/E5.
- **Pre-declared ambiguous cells:** significant vs isotropic null only = pipeline artifact, NOT
  B-evidence; axis-aligned AND persona-occupant simultaneously = report both, resolved only by
  component surgery.

Circumplex-shadow analysis is exploratory with zero gating power.

## Nulls & controls

- **(a) Story-label permutation (decision-grade):** per-story residuals at ~6 layers for all
  17,100 stories; permute labels B = 10,000; recompute every statistic through the identical
  pipeline.
- **(b) Topic-contrast (decision-grade):** ≥ 50 topic-contrast directions
  (cooking/sports/geography from the stories.csv topic column) via the same pipeline; emotion
  statistics must beat the control 95th percentile.
- Isotropic 1/√5120 is reported as a floor only — never a criterion.
- **Comparability gates (void everything if failed):** cos(our empathetic/kind/skeptical
  directions, Lu's matching trait vectors) must clearly beat the matched null; partial out the
  cross-pipeline register direction r = unit(mean neutral-story acts − assistant-default acts);
  report raw and partialed.

## Inference

- All permutation inference goes through `scripts/exact_perm.py::sign_permutation_pvalue`
  (exact enumeration when tractable; the observed statistic is recomputed by the same function as
  the permuted ones; the identity arrangement always counts). The old Monte-Carlo rounding bug
  that produced sub-floor p-values, and its fix, are documented in that file's docstring. No
  prereg cites the retracted numbers.
- Valence-sign tests use the 10 valenced emotion clusters (5 pos / 5 neg): exact enumeration over
  C(10,5) = 252 arrangements; **two-sided exact floor 2/252 ≈ 0.0079**.
- Story-label null: arrangements are intractable for exact enumeration → add-one Monte-Carlo
  (B = 10,000, unrounded statistics); floor 1/10,001 ≈ 1.0e-4.
- Headline threshold: max-T p < 0.005 against BOTH decision-grade nulls.

## Deviations

- **DEVIATION (verified fact):** the cluster's `activations/stories/*.npy` are per-emotion MEANS
  of shape (64, 5120), NOT per-story activations. The story-label-permutation and topic-contrast
  nulls therefore require a new caching pass — `scripts/cache_story_residuals.py` (~1 GPU-h;
  17,100 stories × 64 layers fp16 ≈ 11 GB), being built in parallel by another workstream. E1's
  cosine atlas runs on CPU without it; the two story-nulls are GATED on that cache and the
  B-substrate verdict cannot be issued until they run.
- Local role atlas has 275 roles (not 463); reconciled per Day-0 item 3 before any cosine is read.

## AMENDMENT 1 — 2026-07-04: cos(valence, h_purged) is degenerate; replaced as a primary outcome

*The original text above is preserved unmodified; this amendment supersedes it only where stated.*

### 1. The defect, plainly

The valence axis is defined (Design → Constructions) as positive-minus-negative cluster means —
i.e., a linear combination of the 10 emotion-cluster mean vectors, so **valence lies in
span(v_1..v_10)**. h_purged is built by **linearly projecting that same 10-cluster emotion
subspace out** of every role vector before rebuilding h. A linear projection annihilates
everything in the projected-out span, so h_purged is orthogonal to span(v_1..v_10) by
construction, and therefore

> **cos(valence, h_purged) ≡ 0 — identically, for any possible data.**

The pre-registered sign prediction "B predicts cos(valence, h_purged) > 0" (Design →
Pre-registered signs; Primary outcomes item 1) is **unfalsifiable as written**: no dataset can
produce a non-zero value, and no p-value is defined for a degenerate statistic. The CPU atlas
(`docs/research/e1_atlas_summary.md`) correctly reports it as 0.0000 at all four layers with
"— (degenerate)" in place of an exact p.

### 2. Replacement: the informative B-relevant quantities

The degenerate statistic is **struck as a primary outcome** and replaced by quantities computed
through the identical pipeline:

1. **|cos(h_purged, a)|** — residual alignment between the emotion-purged human sub-axis and the
   assistant axis: the purged-geometry statistic that actually carries B-relevant information.
   Reported at L42 (headline), L32/L50 (Bonferroni ×3), L16 (exploratory), same layer roles as
   the original prereg.
2. **cos(h, h_purged)** — the **contamination measurement**: how much of the raw human sub-axis
   survives emotion purging. Values well below 1 quantify how much of h was built out of the
   emotion subspace; reported raw, no sign prediction.
3. **The two story-cache nulls are the decision-grade B-substrate tests** (unchanged from the
   original Nulls & controls section, now carrying the full decisional weight formerly split with
   the degenerate cosine): **(a) story-label permutation** (per-story residuals, B = 10,000
   add-one Monte-Carlo, floor 1/10,001 ≈ 1.0e-4) and **(b) topic-contrast** (≥ 50 directions,
   emotion statistics must beat the control 95th percentile). The decision rule is otherwise
   unchanged: B-substrate live requires max-T p < 0.005 vs BOTH nulls AND the dose gate; the
   B-substrate verdict remains gated on `scripts/cache_story_residuals.py`.

Unchanged by this amendment: the sign prediction **cos(valence, a) < 0** with its exact
C(10,5) = 252 enumeration (two-sided floor 2/252 ≈ 0.0079); cos(valence, h) stays descriptive
only; occupancy, comparability gates, and the split-injection bridge are untouched.

### 3. Timing: no outcome data influenced this amendment

This amendment is made on **2026-07-04, BEFORE any GPU run** (split-injection bridge and
story-residual cache not yet executed) **and before any E3–E5 data collection**. The defect was
exposed by the CPU-only geometry atlas, but it is a mathematical identity — cos(valence,
h_purged) is zero for *any* data — so its recognition and this replacement depend on no observed
outcome. The decision-grade story-cache nulls promoted above have not been run (still GATED on
the per-story residual cache), and no statistic that could favor A or B has been observed at the
time of this amendment.
