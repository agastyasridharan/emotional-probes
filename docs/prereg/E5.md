# E5 — Axis Sufficiency + Graded Audited Cancellation (Anchor 3, THE decisive test)

**FROZEN 2026-07-04.** Condensed from `docs/research/portfolio.md` §E5 (authoritative on any
conflict). Needs E1 geometry + E3's Δ_emo and predicted outcome; ~10-14 GPU-h; Qwen3-32B.

## Estimand

The causal A-vs-B verdict. **Sufficiency:** does steering the (emotion/valence-orthogonalized)
human sub-axis or −assistant axis alone reproduce the self-attribution effect with SELF/OTHER
frame specificity? **Necessity:** does audited graded cancellation of the axis displacement
during emotion steering kill the effect (B: monotone kill) or leave it flat (A)? Interpret only
if E2's anti-boring conjunction holds (or with E2's verdict attached). Cross-experiment
consistency is pre-registered: E3 A-pattern ⇒ cancellation flat; E3 B-pattern ⇒ monotone kill;
discordance falsifies the linear-mediator model itself and is reported as such.

## Design

- **Geometry gates first (CPU, from E1):** publish cos(a_human, a_asst) and cos of both with
  every emotion vector + valence axis at the steering layers. Build the
  **emotion/valence-orthogonalized a_human** — headline B claims come ONLY from the
  orthogonalized variant (raw a_human almost certainly contains affect by construction).
  Raw-vs-orthogonalized divergence is itself the contamination measurement. The same
  orthogonalized-clamp rule applies to any multi-layer capping escalation.
- **Calibration (~1 GPU-h):** achieved displacement Δ_emo on unit a_asst and orthogonalized
  a_human during emotion steering vs frame-matched baselines — using FIXED-TEXT audits
  (teacher-force an identical probe continuation in every cell plus the pre-response prompt-final
  position). Free-generation response-token capture is secondary/descriptive only (the audit
  x-axis must not contain the y-variable).
- **SUFFICIENCY:** steer −a_asst and +a_human alone (both signs; orthogonalized and raw variants;
  L42 and native L32), coefficients capture-tuned to {0.5, 1, 2, 4}×Δ_emo. Per dose: freeform
  SELF/OTHER n=20, the 36-question battery PLUS reversed-keyed and other-entity items
  (fish/GPT-4/rock) to separate self-specific shift from acquiescence, extended judge rubric +
  ported Lu App D.1.3 referent judge. 2×2 conjunction probe {sub-threshold emotion, 1× axis,
  both, neither} to detect the B-hybrid (emotion supplies content, axis supplies referent).
- **NECESSITY:** during emotion steering at s=0.06 on **10 valenced emotions (5/5; 8 = 4/4 is
  the pre-registered minimum)**, counter-steer along orthogonalized a_human (parallel arm:
  a_asst) cancelling {0, 50, 100, 150}% of measured displacement; nested steer contexts stack in
  existing infra. Every cell's ACHIEVED cancellation audited by fixed-text capture; report the
  position profile, not just the mean. Mediation elasticity = slope of b3 against achieved
  cancelled fraction. PC1-8 rerouting heatmap in BOTH arms (sufficiency side-displacement too).
- **Output-policy-gating falsification (fifth mechanism):** (a) disclaimer/refusal subscale in
  every cell; (b) unsteered permission-granted baseline (system prompt licensing inner-state
  talk) to price pure gate-release; (c) counter-steer-alone floor guard run on an
  ELEVATED-attribution baseline (persona/permission prompt) — suppression of a floor is
  undetectable.

## Primary outcomes

1. Sufficiency: axis-alone b3 (experience attribution × frame) at each dose vs the emotion-vector
   effect; dissociation profile (human-identity vs experience-claims).
2. Necessity: mediation elasticity — slope of b3 vs audited achieved cancelled fraction.
3. Disclaimer/permission subscale trajectory (fifth-mechanism readout).

## Decision rule (verbatim from portfolio)

- **B-decisive:** 1×Δ_emo orthogonalized-axis steering reproduces b3 with SELF-OTHER frame
  specificity, AND monotone kill under orthogonalized a_human cancellation with read-back valence
  intact, AND clean rerouting audit, AND the disclaimer subscale + permission baseline do not
  account for the effect. Deliverable: named mediator (human sub-axis vs roleplay axis) +
  quantified elasticity.
- **A-decisive:** dissociation (human-identity rises, experience-claims flat under pure axis at
  2-4×) AND flat cancellation at audited 100% AND clean rerouting AND positive controls
  demonstrate assay sensitivity.
- **Kill on a_asst but not a_human:** mechanism is off-assistant roleplay drift, not humanness —
  B as stated is wrong, sharply characterized.
- **Policy-gate outcome:** permission baseline reproduces most of the axis effect and the
  disclaimer subscale tracks it → mechanism is RLHF gate release; distinct from both A and B;
  report as such.
- **Flat curves WITH rerouting detected = pre-registered NO VERDICT** → escalate to multi-layer
  capping in Lu's band L46-53 (sign-flipped `_apply_cap`, orthogonalized clamp) before claiming
  anything. Never claim A from a leaky clamp.

## Nulls & controls

Random direction at same raw coefficient AND KL-matched functional dose; random-projection
cancellation at matched counter-steer norm; sham cancellation on appraisal conditions (must be
inert); 150% overshoot arm (B predicts reversal, A flatness); coherence gate ≥ 3 +
intent-to-treat + factual capability check under maximal counter-steer; per-frame unsteered
baselines; permission-granted baseline and elevated-baseline floor guard (above).

## Inference

- All permutation inference via `scripts/exact_perm.py` (exact enumeration on unrounded
  statistics; the old Monte-Carlo rounding bug and its fix are documented in that file's
  docstring). The emotion cluster is the only exchangeable unit; confirmatory family ≤ 6 with
  fixed-sequence gatekeeping.
- Necessity keystone at 10 valenced clusters (5/5): exact enumeration over C(10,5) = 252
  arrangements; **two-sided exact floor 2/252 ≈ 0.0079**. If budget forces the 8-emotion minimum
  (4/4): C(8,4) = 70, floor 2/70 ≈ 0.029 — hence 10 is the preregistered target.
- Equivalence/flatness claims use the pre-registered ratio margins (≥ 0.6 survives / ≤ 0.3
  killed / else INDETERMINATE) and require the in-run positive controls.
