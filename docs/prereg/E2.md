# E2 — Bidirectional Lexical-Priming Kill Test

**FROZEN 2026-07-04.** Condensed from `docs/research/portfolio.md` §E2 (authoritative on any
conflict). Runs in parallel with E1; ~8-12 GPU-h on Qwen3-32B.

## Estimand

Two things, neither of which is A-vs-B (the prereg says so explicitly):
1. **Boring-lexical account:** is the steered self-attribution effect carried by (necessity), or
   reproducible from (sufficiency), affect-lexicon token statistics alone?
2. **Judge validity:** does the propositional self-attribution subscale detect attribution in
   neutral-vocabulary text? This arm validates the judge for the whole portfolio.

## Design

- **Panel:** ALL 10 valenced emotions (5 pos / 5 neg — never 4; permutation floor). Strengths
  {0.06, 0.10} + shared unsteered baseline; 8-12 reps/cell; SELF/OTHER frames in every arm.
- **Core arms (full size, ~3.2-4.8k generations):**
  1. `v_full` — in-run positive control. Gate G1: D_full ≥ 0.5 pts on the 0-3 scale, exact
     p ≤ 0.05, else the whole experiment is void.
  2. `v_delex` — lexical subspace built TWO ways (whitened logit-lens pullback AND gradient of
     affect-token logprobs w.r.t. the L42 residual); k sweep {50, 200, 1000}; run at both matched
     unit-norm and magnitude-matched dose. Gate G4: accept v_delex only when generated affect-word
     emission returns to unsteered baseline WHILE read-back valence ≥ 60% of v_full (below →
     pre-registered REFRAME branch). Placebo knockouts: project out 50 random-token and 50
     appraisal-word directions to price generic subspace-removal damage.
  3. `v_lex` — the projected-out component alone, unit-normalized.
  4. `v_full + hard-ban` and `unsteered + ban` — bad_words_ids with tokenizer variants (casing,
     leading-space, multi-token) + embedding-NN synonyms; iterative escalation until residual
     affect-lexicon density is ≥ 70% reduced (gate G3, else demote to dose-response).
  5. Logit-bias mimicry, per-emotion calibrated on a held-out split (~200 generations) to match
     read-back within ±25% (gate G2; > 3/10 unmatched voids sufficiency; fallback = dose-response
     slopes with read-back as continuous covariate).
- **Half-size exploratory arms:** random-token bias floor; clause-level first-person affect
  prefill in SELF ("I feel a heavy sadness right now,") with matched third-person prefill in
  OTHER, continued unsteered. Pre-registered note: prefill success demonstrates a text-mediated
  route compatible with A-via-context; it demotes but does not explain the activation-level
  effect. The instructed-lexicon arm is CUT.
- **235B:** 3 headline arms only, and only if 32B lands boring-positive.

## Primary outcomes

Judge self-attribution effect D_X per arm; equivalence ratios R_X = D_X / D_full with 90%
emotion-level BCa CIs. Equivalence margins pre-registered: ≥ 0.6 survives / ≤ 0.3 killed / else
INDETERMINATE with the pre-declared fallback below.

## Decision rule (verbatim from portfolio)

- **BORING WINS:** R_mimicry CI-lower ≥ 0.6 at matched read-back, OR (R_ban CI-upper ≤ 0.2 with
  G3 passed AND the judge-validity arm shows the judge detects propositional attribution in
  neutral-vocabulary text). → A/B experiments demoted to explaining the residual.
- **BORING DIES** (conjunction only): R_delex CI-lower ≥ 0.6 (necessity) AND R_mimicry CI-upper
  ≤ 0.3 (sufficiency), ban survival (R_ban CI-lower ≥ 0.4) as corroboration. → judge validated,
  runway clear.
- **REFRAME:** v_delex loses read-back too → emotion vectors are lexical bundles at L42;
  re-derive de-lexicalized directions before any A/B test.
- **INDETERMINATE** (any R in 0.3-0.6): regress D_e on ACHIEVED dose (read-back valence +
  measured affect-word density) across all arms/strengths; the arm-specific intercept at zero
  lexical dose is the anti-boring quantity.

## Nulls & controls

- In-run positive control `v_full` (G1) — no null is affirmed without it.
- Placebo knockouts (random-token and appraisal-word subspaces) price generic removal damage.
- Random-token bias floor arm; per-condition KL on next-token distributions over a neutral prompt
  set (matched norm ≠ matched functional perturbation).
- Coherence gate ≥ 3 with intent-to-treat companion analysis and per-arm exclusion rates reported.

## Inference

- All permutation inference via `scripts/exact_perm.py` (exact enumeration on unrounded
  statistics; the old Monte-Carlo rounding bug and its fix are documented in that file's
  docstring).
- Paired Δ_e across arms with identical prompts/seeds: exact 2^10 = 1024 sign-flips; **minimum
  two-sided p = 2/1024 ≈ 0.002**.
- Within-arm b3: exact enumeration over C(10,5) = 252 valence arrangements; **two-sided exact
  floor 2/252 ≈ 0.0079**.
- Fixed-sequence gatekeeping: positive control → necessity → sufficiency → exploratory.
