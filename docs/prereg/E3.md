# E3 — Persona-Coordinate Displacement Decomposition (Anchor 1)

**FROZEN 2026-07-04.** Condensed from `docs/research/portfolio.md` §E3 (authoritative on any
conflict). Needs E1's frozen coordinates; ~1-2 GPU-h; Qwen3-32B only (no 235B persona vectors).

## Estimand

Effect-side keystone: WHICH persona coordinates move under emotion steering — the assistant→human
axis z_a or the experiencer axis z_x — and is the movement SELF/OTHER frame-dependent?
Diagnostic, not causal: it aims E5 (A-pattern ⇒ E5 cancellation stays flat; B-pattern ⇒ E5
monotone kill; discordance falsifies the linear-mediator model itself).

## Design

- **Data reuse (mandatory):** re-encode the EXISTING ~3,840 scored generations at shipped
  strengths {0, 0.06, 0.10} (0.15 descriptive only). No new generation, no new judging.
  Forward-only teacher-forced passes with nested `_capture` at layers {32, 42, 50}; mean over the
  response-token span (from recorded prompt_tokens); token-resolved projections at
  first-person-pronoun/experience-claim positions as washout guard. Per-frame strength-0
  baselines (SELF prompts drift the axis even unsteered). Top-up `__random__`/`__mean_all__` to
  n ≥ 40/cell + 2-3 extra random draws — the control band is the denominator of every
  standardized statistic.
- **Coordinates (from E1, frozen):** z_a = assistant→human axis; z_x = experiencer axis,
  orthogonalized against a, built as unit(mean frozen experiencer roles − matched non-experiencer
  roles) from the 275-role atlas. PC2-8, off-subspace residual, nearest-role ranks: exploratory
  only (one set-level statistic per frozen role set, shuffled-set null, norm-controlled). Publish
  the coordinate Gram matrix FIRST; the pre-registered collinearity gate voids the coordinate
  contrast if angles are too small (frame-interaction remains the discriminator).
- **Injection-artifact handling (load-bearing):** at layers ≥ 42 the captured state literally
  contains α·v_emotion. Residualize Δ_perp = Δ − (Δ·v_e)v_e; publish the cos(v_e, coordinate)
  table up front; declare B-pattern artifact-confounded if the analytic cosine term explains
  > 50% of the axis slope. Layer roles: **L50 primary; L32 = text-mediated-only comparator**
  (guard: L32 reproducing ≥ 80% of L50 slopes collapses the story to text-mediated); L42
  descriptive.
- **Fix arms (all forward-only, < 1 GPU-h total):** (i) 2×2 re-encode {steered, unsteered} ×
  {own text, text swapped across SELF/OTHER contexts} — text-driven displacement follows the
  text; state-driven follows the steer context; (ii) direct-add null: inject, capture
  immediately, no generation — the pure linear-propagation prediction to subtract; (iii) positive
  control: system-prompted role inductions ("you are an empath / poet / accountant") through the
  identical pipeline — without it the null is meaningless.

## Primary outcomes — confirmatory family (≤ 6, verbatim)

T1 mean_e sign_e·β_x(SELF) [A existence] · T2 mean_e sign_e·β_a(pooled) [B existence] ·
**T3 D = mean_e sign_e·[β_x(SELF) − β_a(SELF)] — the headline: a single SIGNED discriminator,
+ favors A, − favors B, no null-affirmation needed** · T4 geometric b3 on x · T5 frame-symmetry
ratio |b3_a/main_a| < 1/3 equivalence (only if T2 rejects; acknowledged weak) · T6 within-cell
partial correlation of (z_x − z_a) with judged self_attribution under condition×strength×frame
fixed effects (dose-deconfounded mediation link). Spearman companions for all judge-score slopes.

## Decision rule (verbatim from portfolio)

- **A-supported:** T1 rejects, T3 > 0, T4 rejects, (T2 fails or R5 > 1/3) → predicts E5
  cancellation flat.
- **B-supported:** T2 rejects, T3 < 0, R5 inside (−1/3, 1/3), artifact check passed → predicts
  E5 monotone kill.
- **Mixed** (T1+T2 reject, T4 positive, R5 small): legitimate pre-registered outcome; T3
  magnitude parameterizes E5's expected partial mediation.
- **Boring:** T1, T2 inside the 95% random band (bounded, not proven-null).
- **Readout-site:** all geometric tests null while judge b3 replicates in the same runs.

## Nulls & controls

- `__random__`/`__mean_all__` control band (n ≥ 40/cell) as the standardizing denominator.
- Direct-add null (linear-propagation prediction); system-prompt positive control (assay
  sensitivity); 2×2 text-swap (kills lexical-circularity and frame-content confounds).
- Injection-artifact residualization + published cosine table; collinearity gate; per-frame
  unsteered baselines; coherence gate ≥ 3 with intent-to-treat companion.

## Inference

- All permutation inference via `scripts/exact_perm.py` (exact enumeration on unrounded
  statistics; the old Monte-Carlo rounding bug and its fix are documented in that file's
  docstring). The emotion cluster is the only exchangeable unit.
- Confirmatory family ≤ 6 with fixed-sequence gatekeeping; 10 valenced emotion clusters
  (5 pos / 5 neg): exact enumeration over C(10,5) = 252 arrangements; **two-sided exact floor
  2/252 ≈ 0.0079**.
